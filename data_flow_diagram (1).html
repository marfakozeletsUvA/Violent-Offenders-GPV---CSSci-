03 EDA Findings
Distributions & Target Variable
Target · Overdispersion
Variance ≫ Mean
Journalist killings: high zero-inflation, heavy right tail. Variance/mean ratio confirms overdispersion → negative binomial regression required, not Poisson.
Predictors · Skewness
Log Transforms Needed
Arms transfers, ODA, debt, GDP, population all highly right-skewed (skewness > 5). v2x_polyarchy approximately uniform on [0,1].

Outliers & Handling
Not errors
Real Extreme Events
Russia→India arms deals, USA→Ukraine/Iraq aid, Iraq War journalist deaths. IQR method flags 10–18% but all are substantively meaningful.
Strategy
Keep + Transform
Retain all outliers (they ARE the phenomenon). Log transforms reduce leverage. Sensitivity checks: models with/without top 1%.
