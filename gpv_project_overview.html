Neo-Colonial Economic Score (Designed, Not Yet Implemented)

A composite predictor capturing both trade exposure and structural asymmetry between sender and recipient — operationalizing economic neo-colonialism at the dyad level.

econ_neocol_score = trade_dependency × income_asymmetry
trade_dependency = bilateral_trade / receiver_GDP
income_asymmetry = sender_income_code − receiver_income_code
High Income = 4
Upper-Middle = 3
Lower-Middle = 2
Low Income = 1

Historical income classifications from World Bank OGHIST.xlsx. Trade data source pending — BACI (CEPII) preferred over IMF IMTS: better coverage (200 countries, 1995–2024), already reconciles mirror flow discrepancies. 3-year gap (1992–1994) is a documented limitation.
